Our Story

Building South Africa's premier electronics destination

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Cape Town, TechHub SA began as a small electronics repair shop with a big vision. What started as a passion project by tech enthusiast Thabo Mthembu has grown into one of South Africa's most trusted electronics retailers.

Our journey began when Thabo noticed the gap in quality electronics retail in South Africa. Too often, customers had to choose between high prices or questionable quality. TechHub SA was created to bridge that gap, offering premium electronics at competitive prices with unmatched customer service.

Today, we serve customers across all nine provinces, from our flagship store in Cape Town to our growing online presence. We're proud to be a 100% South African owned business, contributing to our local economy while bringing the latest technology to South African consumers.

Thabo Mthembu

Founder & CEO

Born and raised in Johannesburg, Thabo moved to Cape Town to study Computer Science at the University of Cape Town. His passion for technology and entrepreneurship led him to establish TechHub SA after recognizing the need for better electronics retail in South Africa.

When not running the business, Thabo enjoys hiking Table Mountain, supporting the Springboks, and mentoring young entrepreneurs in the Western Cape tech community.
